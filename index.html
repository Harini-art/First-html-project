Pushpa 2: The Rule, directed by Sukumar, continues the story of Pushpa Raj (Allu Arjun) as he solidifies his power as the kingpin of the red sanders smuggling syndicate, while facing intense opposition. After establishing his dominance in the first film, Pushpa now lives a life of luxury with his wife, Srivalli (Rashmika Mandanna), but his rule is challenged by two primary threats. The second threat emerges when Pushpa, insulted by the state's Chief Minister, manipulates the political landscape by backing a new CM candidate with help from an international smuggler, escalating the conflict from the local forest to the realm of state politics. The film delves into Pushpa's character and his pursuit of respect, connecting his ruthless actions to the trauma of his illegitimate birth.
